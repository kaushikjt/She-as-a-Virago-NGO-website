COVID_19 EMERGENCY RELIEF FUNDS-

The second wave of the pandemic has hit people really hard. And with another lockdown many people in our society are struggling to make ends meet. They are struggling with no food, money and most importantly with no confidence. Now, it is our duty to help. So we at She As A Virago are trying to fix the problems faced by homeless women and domestic workers. While we are actively attempting to raise funds, we need your help to make our efforts go even further. Your support is crucial to us. If you are able, we'd love if you could make a donation for the cause to help us achieve our mission to help 50 families with commodities for a month.
We all know it is a crucial period after seeing distressing images the need for oxygen cylinders and beds. But if we are not in a position to give 5000 for a bed or cylinders, let us atleast do our part by giving 500 to feed people suffering from hunger. Your donations will go towards preparing ration kits, each worth of Rs.1000. You can donate multiple kits as well. A single kit contains Rice, Dal, atta,oil, biscuits, essential spices, tea powder,sanitary napkins and reusable masks.
We sincerely request you to contribute generously to feed someone today.
