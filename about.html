ABOUT US
She As A Virago is a student led Non-Profitable community working towards making Girls more confident to rasie their voice against the Ills and stand for what is Right.
It all started in 2018 with Three members ,Where we started creating Awareness through articles and stories within our close circules. later through Skits with a Team of 5. Now our Virago Team consists of 30 Members .

For the past years where the covid pandameic has struck the diverse,we worked with students and came up with solutions to fight the virus and were successful in executing a lot of their solutions through our Fundraiser ,Conferences,webinars and more.
